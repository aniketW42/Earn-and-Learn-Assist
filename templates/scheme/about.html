About the Scheme

Empowering students through the dignity of work and learning

Karmveer Bhaurao Patil Earn & Learn Scheme

Established 1971

by Padmashri Dr. Vitthalrao Vikhe Patil

The Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil Earn and Learn Scheme was initiated with the main objective of helping poor and needy students pursue higher education independently.

The scheme aims to inculcate self-discipline and the dignity of labor among students, while also developing them as multifaceted personalities with academic excellence and a commitment to an egalitarian society.

The scheme provides financial assistance to students from economically backward rural areas who are intelligent and meritorious, enabling them to continue their education without financial burden.

Core Values & Benefits

Financial Support

Provides necessary financial assistance to cover educational expenses for economically disadvantaged students.

Academic Excellence

Encourages and enables students to maintain focus on their academic performance while gaining work experience.

Dignity of Labor

Instills the value and dignity of work through practical experience in various institutional departments.

Community Building

Fosters a commitment to creating an egalitarian society through service and personal development.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students from economically backward rural areas
  • Demonstrated academic merit and potential
  • Willingness to participate in institutional work
  • Regular attendance in academic programs
